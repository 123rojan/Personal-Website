About Me

Engineer. Researcher.
Educator.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Khwopa College of Engineering, Tribhuvan University, where my thesis examined the effect of current harmonic-induced heating on transformer ageing — work that earned an Outstanding Paper Award at RESSD-2025.

My research interests sit at the intersection of power electronics and advanced control: solid-state transformers, Model Predictive Control (MPC), Virtual Synchronous Generator (VSG) modelling, Modular Multilevel Converters (MMC), and grid-forming inverter strategies for sustainable energy systems.

Beyond research, I actively contribute to engineering education — having delivered training programmes in MATLAB, LaTeX, and AutoCAD to junior students, and currently serving as Teaching Assistant in the Department of Electrical Engineering.

I am actively seeking graduate-level research opportunities in power electronics, control systems, and energy infrastructure.
